Meet the Creator

Gabriel Hangad, Creator of I-STORIES

Gabriel Hangad A.K.A CreamO is the person behind the I-STORIES website, a platform filled with imagination, based on Gab's real experiences. He aims to create a space where readers, particularly Filipinos, can enjoy stress-relieving stories in Tagalog, Cebuano, and English. Combining "I" for "Me" and "STORIES" for both "Imaginary" or real, this website is a creative outlet inspired by GENDESU.

More About This Website

Gabriel loves reading manga, wattpad, and more. This website reflects his passion and dream of becoming a writer, creating a community for fellow enthusiasts. Although he's a beginner, he believes that through perseverance, he will achieve his dreams. The website is not just about sharing stories, but also about fostering a supportive environment for writers.
