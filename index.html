Gabriel Hangad, Creator of I-STORIES

Gabriel Hangad is the person behind the I-STORIES website,
a website that's all about imagination, and based on Gab's
real experience. He wanted to create a place where the reader/s
(filipinos) could enjoy and relieve there stress by reading
I-STORIES and not hard to read for them (tagalog, cebuano, english).
I means "Me" and STORIES means "Imaginary" or real,
if you combine the two it means "My Imagination" this also
inspired by GENDESU.
